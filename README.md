## ğŸ” Key Findings & Insights

### **Regional Happiness Patterns**
- **Highest Average Happiness**: Northern Europe and North America
- **Lowest Average Happiness**: Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia
- **Most Consistent Happiness**: Western European countries

### **Economic Impact Analysis**
- **Strong Correlation**: GDP per capita significantly influences happiness scores
- **Diminishing Returns**: Beyond certain GDP levels, additional wealth has less impact on happiness
- **Top Performers**: Nordic countries maintain high happiness despite varying GDP trends

### **Social & Lifestyle Factors**
- **Critical Factors**: Social support and freedom of choice are paramount
- **Health Importance**: Healthy life expectancy strongly correlates with happiness
- **Generosity Impact**: Less significant than other factors but contributes to overall well-being

### **Country-Specific Insights**
- **Most Happy Countries**: Finland, Denmark, Iceland consistently top rankings
- **Least Happy Countries**: Afghanistan, South Sudan show consistent challenges
- **Improving Nations**: Several Eastern European countries show positive trends
